Overview

Modonix partnered with a mid-sized U.S. distributor whose pricing decisions were inconsistent, reactive, and disconnected from true profitability. Despite healthy revenue, margins fluctuated unpredictably, discounts were handled manually, and product-level profitability was unclear. The result? Missed profit opportunities, inconsistent pricing logic, and a leadership team forced to rely on instinct instead of structured financial insight.

Modonix built a margin-driven decision system that unified pricing logic, clarified product-level profitability, and empowered the team to make faster, smarter pricing decisions rooted in data not guesswork.

Within 60 days, the client gained complete transparency into SKU-level margins, contribution impact, and pricing thresholds unlocking a more stable, predictable, and profitable pricing engine.

A fast-growing distributor approached Modonix with a familiar problem: revenue looked strong on paper, but real profit margins were unstable, inconsistent, and difficult to measure. Pricing decisions were being made reactively based on gut feeling, competitor behavior, or one-off customer demands.

Opportunity

The result: A business that appeared healthy at the top line but leaked profit at the SKU level quietly and constantly.

During the diagnostic phase, Modonix identified multiple structural gaps that created an immediate opportunity for improvement:

  • Inconsistent pricing logic across departments
  • Poor tracking of contribution margin and true profitability
  • No safeguard to prevent low-margin or negative-margin orders
  • Difficulty adjusting pricing in response to supplier increases
  • Leadership forced to rely on spreadsheets, instincts, and delayed reporting
  • No standardized margin thresholds, creating room for a tiered pricing model

Strategy Overview

Rather than “fixing prices,” the true opportunity was to build a unified margin intelligence system one that empowered the team to understand, control, and strategically grow profitability.

Modonix implemented a system-first, margin-first framework designed to modernize pricing strategy, unify financial insight, and automate decision-making logic.

The approach focused on creating clarity, structure, and operational discipline, replacing guesswork with repeatable models and data-driven pricing execution.

Execution

Execution centered on building a disciplined margin intelligence system, not cosmetic dashboards. The goal: replace fragmented spreadsheets with a unified operational engine for pricing.

We built a SKU-level model that calculates: • Landed cost • Contribution margin • Margin floors • Margin ceilings • Freight-adjusted profitability • Discount impact This became the backbone of the pricing system. Pricing Framework Standardization We implemented a tiered pricing structure: • Strategic products • High-margin leaders • Competitive match SKUs • Protected margin items • Loss-leader visibility This created clarity across sales, operations, and leadership. Guardrail System Implementation We created automated triggers that highlight: • Quotes below approved margin • Negative-margin orders • Margin erosion from supplier increases • Customers exceeding discount thresholds This eliminated accidental losses and increased accountability. Reporting & Insight Layer We built a leadership dashboard showing: • Real-time margin performance • Contribution margin by segment • Pricing opportunities • Product groups underperforming • Top profitable SKUs + bottom drivers Instant insight replaced delayed reporting. Cross Department Alignment Sales, finance, and operations were aligned around one pricing language and margin structure removing guesswork and communication friction. Results Within 60 days, the business gained clarity, confidence, and a measurable lift in profitability driven purely by margin intelligence, not additional revenue.
